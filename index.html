About Us

HK Architects is a premier architecture, construction, and interior design firm with a strong presence across South India. We bring together visionary design and meticulous execution to create spaces that inspire and endure.

With a team of experienced architects and engineers, we have successfully delivered over 600 interior projects, establishing ourselves as leaders in precision construction and design excellence.
